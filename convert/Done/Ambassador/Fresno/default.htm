Environmental Ambassadors

Fresno Unified School District
District Web Site

Current Status

  • Strong energy efficiency and education component.
  • Recycling dumpsters are located at every school and office in the district.
  • District currently recycles paper (including colored paper), chipboard, magazines, corrugated cardboard, steel and aluminum cans, and #1, 2, and 3 plastic containers.
  • Program also includes green waste, used oil, antifreeze, lead batteries, and waste paint thinner.
  • Environmental education efforts are strong but must be fully integrated into standards-based curriculum.
