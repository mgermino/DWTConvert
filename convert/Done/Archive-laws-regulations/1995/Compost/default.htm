Composting Operations and Facilities

These regulations were approved by the Office of Administrative Law on June 30, 1995 and became effective July 30, 1995.

Description
This rulemaking provided permitting guidelines for green material, food material, sewage sludge, and mixed solid waste composting operations and facilities.

Affected Regulatory Code Sections:
Title 14, California Code of Regulations (CCR), Division 7, Chapter 3.1, Articles 1 through 9, Sections 17850 through 17895.

History
These regulations were approved by OAL on June 30, 1995 and became effective July 30, 1995.
