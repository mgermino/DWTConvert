Contaminated Soils: Standardized Permit

Description: This rulemaking relocated the Standardized Contaminated Soil Solid Waste Facilities Permit (CIWMB Form 90), which is referenced in and incorporated into the regulatory language in Title 14, California Code of Regulations, Division 7, Sections 17362.3 and 17363, from its previous location in Appendix CSSP, without modification, to Appendix "A" at the end of Division 7. The language of Sections 17362.3 and 17363 was revised to delete reference to Appendix CSSP, incorporate form CIWMB 90 by reference, and refer to Appendix A.

Affected Regulatory Code Sections:  Title 14, California Code of Regulations (CCR), Division 7,  Sections 17362.3 and 17363,  Form 90.

Status:  The rulemaking was approved by OAL on November 4, 1996 and became effective on December 4, 1996.
