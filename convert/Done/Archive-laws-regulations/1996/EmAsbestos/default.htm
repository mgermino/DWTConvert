Asbestos-Containing Waste Emergency Regulations

Description:  This rulemaking required an owner or operator of a non-Class I landfill that accepts asbestos containing waste to meet the requirements of this regulation. The regulation makes operation conditional on two requirements. The first condition is that the landfill has obtained WDRs from the Regional Water Quality Control Board that allow the disposal of asbestos containing waste. Second, the landfill must meet the specific conditions listed in the regulation.

Affected Regulatory Code Sections:  Title 14, California Code of Regulations (CCR), Division 7, Chapter 3.5, Sections 17897 - 17897.25.

History:  These emergency regulations were approved by OAL on August 1, 1996 and became effective on that same date.
