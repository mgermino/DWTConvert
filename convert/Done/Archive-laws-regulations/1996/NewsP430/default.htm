Modifications to CIWMB Form 430 A, Section 100

Description: This rulemaking made certain format and instructional modifications to CIWMB Form 430, Newsprint Consumer Certification.

Affected Regulatory Code Sections: Title 14, California Code of Regulations (CCR), Appendix A, Form 430.

History: This Section 100 rulemaking was approved by the Office of Administrative Law in May 1996, and became effective 30 days later.

Rulemaking Documents:

Memo to OAL (Word 6.0/95, 15KB)
Memorandum to the Office of Administrative Law requesting approval of the change, which had no regulatory effect.

Appendix A to OAL Memo (Word 6.0/95, 16KB)
Justification for using Section 100 (change without regulatory effect) as a mechanism for this regulatory change.
