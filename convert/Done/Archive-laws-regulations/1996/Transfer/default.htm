Transfer Operations

Description:  The regulations define transfer operations, place the operations into the California Integrated Waste Management Board's (CIWMB) regulatory tiers, and establish permitting requirements and minimum operating standards.

Affected Regulatory Code Sections: Title 14, California Code of Regulations (CCR), Division 7, Chapter 3, Articles 6.0, 6.1, 6.2, and 6.3,  Sections 17400 through 17414;     Article 6,  Article 6.4,  Sections 17413  and 17420; and amending the title of Chapter 5.

History: The regulations were approved by OAL on October 11, 1996 and became effective on the same date.

Contact: Beatrice Poroli, (916) 341-6411
