Asbestos-Containing Waste Certificate of Compliance

Description:  This rulemaking created a permit, inspection and enforcement program for the disposal of asbestos-containing waste at solid facilities.

Affected Regulatory Code Sections:  Title 14, California Code of Regulations (CCR), Division 7, Chapter 3.5, Articles 1 through 4, Sections 17897 through 17897.25.

History:  The regulations were approved by OAL on January 13, 1997 and became effective on February 13, 1997.
