Nonhazardous Ash

Description:  This rulemaking defines nonhazardous ash operations and facilities, placed the operations and facilities into the regulatory tiers, and established permitting requirements and minimum operating standards to protect public health and safety and the environment.

Affected Regulatory Code Sections:  Title 14, California Code of Regulations, Division 7, Chapter 3; adopt new Article 5.8, sections 17375 through 17379.1; and amend Chapter 5, Article 3.2, by adopting a new section 18226.

History: These regulations were approved by OAL on September 26, 1997 and became effective on that date.
