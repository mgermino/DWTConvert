Composting: Chippers and Grinders Emergency Regulations

Description:  The emergency regulations required owners or operators of chipping and grinding or storage activities with 1,000 cubic yards of organic material on site at any one time to meet minimum fire safety, nuisance control, and record-keeping requirements.

Affected Regulatory Code Sections:  Title 14, California Code of Regulations (CCR), Division 7, Chapter 3.1, Sections 17850 - 17870.

History: These emergency regulations were approved by OAL on April 7, 1997 and became effective on that same date.
