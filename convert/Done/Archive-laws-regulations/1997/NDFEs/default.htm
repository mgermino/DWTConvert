Nondisposal Facility Element (NDFE)

Description:  The regulations provide procedures for the preparation of Nondisposal Facility Elements.

Affected Regulatory Code Sections:  Title 14, California Code of Regulations (CCR), Division 7, Chapter 9, Article 6.4, Sections 18752 - 18754.5

History:   These regulations were approved by OAL February 13, 1997 and filed with the Secretary of State on that date.
