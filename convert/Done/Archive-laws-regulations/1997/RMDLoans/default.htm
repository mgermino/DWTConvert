RMDZ Loan Program

Description:  The Recycling Market Development Zone (RMDZ) Loan Program provides direct loans to businesses that use post consumer or secondary waste materials to manufacture new products. To be eligible, the business must be located in a designated Recycling Market Development Zone. This rulemaking included various amendments to the existing program regulations in order to clarify, simplify, and improve the administration of the program and  to accelerate the loan application, review, and approval process.

Affected Regulatory Code Sections: Title 14, California Code of Regulations (CCR), Division 7, Chapter 4, Article 1.1, sections 17930 through 17935.6. 

Status:  Proposed regulations were adopted on May 28, 1997  and filed with OAL on August 1, 1997.  Major portions of the regulations were approved by OAL on September 15, 1997. The remainder of the proposed regulations--primarily clarifying language and description of the loan application review and approval process--were revised and issued for an additional 15-day comment period, which ends November 4, 1997. Staff anticipates that these revisions will be submitted for approval at the November 6, 1997 Market Development Committee meeting and at the November 19, 1997 Board Meeting.
