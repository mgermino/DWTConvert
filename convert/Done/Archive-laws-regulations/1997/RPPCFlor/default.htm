Rigid Plastic Packaging Containers: Floral Industry

Description:  The amendments added a new option for complying with California's rigid plastic packaging container recycling requirements for manufacturers of floral preservatives, as required by Chapter 511, Statutes of 1996 (AB 2508, House). The amendments also clarify how the product manufacturer will certify compliance to the Board under the new two-year reuse compliance option.

Affected Regulatory Code Sections:  Title 14, California Code of Regulations (CCR), Division 7, Chapter 4, Article 3 sections 17944 and 17945.6.

History:  These amendments were approved by OAL on May 30, 1997 and filed with the Secretary of State on that date.
