Chipping, Grinding, and Vermicomposting

Description: The regulations clarify the vermicomposting exclusion, establish minimum standards for storage and chipping and grinding activities, and regulate stabilized compost which has not been sold, bagged for sale, or beneficially used.  For text of the regulations, please see below under "Documents."

Affected Regulatory Code Sections: Title 14, California Code of Regulations, Division 7, Chapter 3.1; adopt sections 17852, 17855(a)(6), & 17855(a)(7), 17862.1, and 17862.2; amend 17850, 17852, 17855, 17856, 17857, 17858, 17859, 17863, 17867, 17868.1, 17868.2, 17868.3, and 17869.

History: The CIWMB adopted these permanent regulations at their August 27, 1997 meeting. The rulemaking file was submitted to the Office of Administrative Law (OAL) on November 25, 1997. The OAL approved the regulations on January 9, 1998 and they became immediately effective on the approval date.
