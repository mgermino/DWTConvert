Recycled-Content Newsprint Certification Penalty

The proposed regulations were approved by the Office of Administrative Law (OAL) on December 29, 1999 and became effective January 21, 2000.

Description 
Modification of the recycled-content newsprint regulations (14 CCR 17950-17974) to implement a penalty structure for late filers and nonfilers, making specific the civil penalty provision in Public Resources Code section 42791. The regulations affect late and delinquent filers for the 1999 compliance year and forward.

Affected Regulatory Code Sections  
Title 14, California Code of Regulations (CCR), Division 7, Chapter 4, Article 4, section 17974

History
The change was approved by the Board at its February 23, 1999 meeting. Notice of a 45-day public review and comment period was published in the August 6, 1999 issue of the California Regulatory Notice Register. Comments were accepted until September 20, 1999. The Board held a public hearing and adopted the proposed regulations at its September 21-22, 1999 meeting in Sacramento. The proposed regulations were approved by the Office of Administrative Law (OAL) on December 29, 1999 and became effective January 21, 2000.
