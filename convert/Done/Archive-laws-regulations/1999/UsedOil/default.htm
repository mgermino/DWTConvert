Used Oil Recycling Program

 
Workshops were held in October 1999, but the formal rulemaking process was never begun. Board staff is currently awaiting the outcome of a comprehensive program assessment to revise the Used Oil Program regulations. The final report will be presented in the summer of 2003. Existing regulations are in place.

Description:
The proposed regulations were intended to streamline the Used Oil Recycling Program requirements, provide more clarity to regulated entities and address changes in the law. The proposed regulations were changed from a "question/answer" to a standard regulation format. Also, many sections would be renumbered and numerous editorial and insignificant changes would also be made. The proposed regulations will replace the existing regulations after a formal rulemaking process.

Affected Regulatory Code Sections:
Title 14, California Code of Regulations, Division 7, sections 18600-18659.5

History
A draft of the proposed changes was posted on the Internet in September 1999. Board staff held workshops in October 1999 to outline the changes and receive informal comments. Staff requested Board approval to begin the formal rulemaking process at its December 14, 1999 meeting, but the formal rulemaking process was never begun. Board staff is currently awaiting the outcome of a comprehensive program assessment to revise the Used Oil Program regulations. The final report will be presented in the summer of 2003.
