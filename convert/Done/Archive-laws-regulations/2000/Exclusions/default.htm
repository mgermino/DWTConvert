Waste Tire Storage Facilities

Description: 
The proposed waste tire storage exclusion regulations (WTSE) will make permanent the emergency regulations that were approved by the Office of Administrative Law on June 16, 1998. Those emergency regulations removed the indoor storage, "recycling business," and "general" exclusions from Title 14 California Code of Regulations, Section 18420.

Affected Code Sections: 
Portions of Title 14, California Code of Regulations (CCR), Division 7, Chapter 3 and Chapter 6.

Status:
Completion of the rulemaking process on the existing emergency regulations was delayed while the Board considered the option of adding other changes to tire regulations, based on its report to the Governor and Legislature, as required by AB 117 (Escutia, 1998). At its July 27, 1999 meeting the Board authorized staff to proceed with the rulemaking process to make the emergency regulations permanent. Notice of the 45-day public comment period was published in the California Regulatory Notice Register from October 29, 1999 to December 13, 1999.

At its December 15, 1999 meeting, the Board adopted the Waste Tire Storage Exclusion Regulations and directed staff to submit the regulations to the Office of Administrative Law for its review and approval.
