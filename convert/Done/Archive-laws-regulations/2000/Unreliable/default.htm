Unreliable Contractors

The Office of Administrative Law approved the regulations on January 7, 2000.

Description
The regulations determine those actions the Board considers unreliable, so potential contractors, grantees and loan applicants will know in advance whether they are reliable and can enter into an agreement with the Board.

Affected Regulatory Code Sections  
Title 14, California Code of Regulations (CCR), Division 7, Chapter 1, Article 5, Sections 17050-17063

Rulemaking History  
A 45-day public comment period was noticed in the January 1, 1999 issue of the California Regulatory Notice Register.  The written comment period closed February 16, 1998.  At the March 23-24 meeting, the Board directed staff to make specified changes to the regulations and notice them for an additional 15-day comment period. Following the 15-day comment period, the Board adopted the regulations on May 27, 1999.  The rulemaking file was submitted to OAL on November 24, 1999 and approved on January 7, 2000.
