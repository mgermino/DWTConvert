Rigid Plastic Packaging Container Recycling Rates

The Office of Administrative Law (OAL) approved emergency regulations and filed them with the Secretary of State on September 17, 2001, at which time the regulations became effective.

Description
Use of previous year's recycling rates for compliance in current calendar year.

Affected Regulatory Code Sections
Title 14, California Code of Regulations, Division 7, Chapter 4, Section 17943

History
At its June 19-20, 2001 meeting, the Board directed staff to pursue emergency regulations to use the previous year's recycling rates for compliance in the current calendar year.

The Board adopted the emergency regulations at its July 25-26, 2001 meeting.

The OAL approved the emergency regulations and filed them with the Secretary of State on September 17, 2001, at which time the regulations became effective.
