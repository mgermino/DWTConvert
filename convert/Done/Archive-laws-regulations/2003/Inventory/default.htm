Inventory of Solid Waste Facilities That Violate State Minimum Standards

 
The Office of Administrative Law approved the regulations on March 5, 2003. The regulations are effective on April 4, 2003.

Description
New regulations to establish the process for inclusion on inventory of solid waste facilities that violate state minimum standards.

Affected Regulatory Code Sections
Title 14, California Code of Regulations, Division 7, Chapter 5, Article 5.1, Sections 18360-18368

Rulemaking History
As directed by the Board, staff held public workshops on March 7, 2001 (San Bernardino, California), March 8, 2001 (Truckee, California), and March 12, 2001 (Sacramento, California), to solicit comments on the effectiveness of the inventory and the need for regulations to enhance implementation.  Staff conducted public workshops on November 28, 2001, in Diamond Bar, California and on November 30, 2001, in Sacramento, California to solicit comments on draft regulatory text.

At its January 22-23, 2002 meeting the Board directed staff to begin the formal rulemaking process by publishing a notice for a 45-day comment period. The 45-day public comment period ran from July 5 through August 19, 2002. The Board conducted a public hearing on September 9, 2002.

At its October 7, 2002 meeting the Permitting and Enforcement Committee directed staff to make specified changes and to begin a 15-day public comment period. The comment period ran from October 24 through November 8, 2002.

The Board adopted the regulations at its November 19-20, 2002 meeting. The Office of Administrative Law approved the regulations on March 5, 2003.  The regulations are effective on April 4, 2003.
