Landfill Closure Loan Program
On May 18, 2004 the Office of Administrative Law approved the regulations and filed them with the Secretary of State. The regulations are effective as of June 17, 2004.

Description
New regulations for the administration of a landfill closure loan program.

Affected Regulatory Code Sections:
Title 27, California Code of Regulations, Division 2, Chapter 8; Subchapter 1, Article 1, Sections 23001 through 23014

Rulemaking History
At its April 7, 2002 meeting the Board's Permitting and Enforcement Committee directed staff to initiate a 45-day public comment period for the proposed regulations. A 45-day comment period ran from September 12 through October 27, 2003.

The Board conducted a public hearing at the November 3, 2003 Permitting and Enforcement Committee meeting.

The Board adopted the regulations at its February 18, 2004 meeting. Staff submitted the final rulemaking file to the Office of Administrative Law on April 6, 2004.

On May 18, 2004 the Office of Administrative Law approved the regulations and filed them with the Secretary of State. The regulations are effective as of June 17, 2004.
