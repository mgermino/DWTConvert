Application for Solid Waste Facility Permit/Waste Discharge Requirements
On December 13, 2004 the Office of Administrative Law approved the regulations and filed them with the Secretary of State. The regulations are effective as of January 1, 2005.

Description
Revision of joint application form CIWMB E-1-77 used for Solid Waste Facility Permits and Waste Discharge Requirements.

Affected Code Sections
Title 27, California Code of Regulations (CCR), Division 2, Appendix 1, and Section 21570

Rulemaking History
At its April 7, 2002 meeting the Board's Permitting and Enforcement Committee directed staff to initiate a 45-day public comment period for the proposed regulations. The comment period ran from February 27 through April 12, 2004. At its May 12, 2004 meeting the Board directed staff to initiate an additional 15-day public comment period for changes to the proposed regulations. The comment period ran from July 15-30, 2004.

The Board adopted the regulations at its August 17, 2004 meeting. On October 27, 2004, Board staff submitted the final rulemaking file to the Office of Administrative Law for 30 working day review. On December 13, 2004 the Office of Administrative Law approved the regulations and filed them with the Secretary of State. The regulations are effective as of January 1, 2005.
