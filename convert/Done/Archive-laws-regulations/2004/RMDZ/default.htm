Proposed Regulations

Recycling Market Development Zone Designation

These regulations were approved by the Office of Administrative Law on September 7, 2004 and filed with the Secretary of State. The regulations will become effective as of October 7, 2004.

Description
Clarifies and streamlines the designation process for Recycling Market Development Zones.

Affected Regulatory Code Sections
Title 14, California Code of Regulations; Division 7, California Integrated Waste Management Board; Chapter 4, Resource Conservation Programs; Article 1, Recycling Market Development Zone Designation Process

Stakeholder Input

November 24, 2003. A public hearing to receive comments on the proposed rulemaking was held on November 24, 2003 in Sacramento. See the Notice of Proposed Rulemaking (below) for more information.

Rulemaking History
Staff completed a draft of revised regulations governing the designation of Recycling Market Development Zones. The draft was circulated to Recycling Market Development Zone Administrators for their informal review and comments.

A 45-day comment period ran until 5:00 p.m. on Monday, December 29, 2003. The CIWMB also accepted oral and written comments during the November 24, 2003 public hearing described above under Stakeholder Input.

A second 45-day comment period ended on Monday, April 12, 2004. One multipart comment was received from the Department of Finance during this period and staff responded appropriately.

The Board approved the proposed regulations at its June 15-16, 2004 meeting (see agenda item 13). The regulations were approved by the Office of Administrative Law on September 7, 2004 and are effective as of October 7, 2004.
