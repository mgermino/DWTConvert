Waste Tire Hauler Registration and Manifesting Requirements for Retreaders
On August 23, 2004 the Office of Administrative Law approved these emergency regulations and filed them with the Secretary of State. They became effective immediately and are effective through December 31, 2004.

Description
Revision of existing waste tire hauler registration and manifest regulations to allow retreaders to self-certify with the CIWMB and to allow self-certified retreaders to use a new Retreader Trip Log and to substitute customer invoices for the Manifest Forms currently required. The proposed changes implement, interpret and make specific the provisions of Chapter 838, Statutes of 2000 (Escutia, SB 876), as well as correct errors, add language to make the regulations more functional, and delete unnecessary language.

Affected Regulatory Code Sections
Title 14, California Code of Regulations, Division 7, Chapter 6, Article 8.5

Rulemaking History
At the June 15-16, 2004 Board meeting the Board voted to adopt emergency regulations which would revise the Waste Tire Hauler Manifesting requirements for retreaders.

On August 23, 2004 the Office of Administrative Law approved the regulations and filed them with the Secretary of State. The regulations became effective immediately and are effective through December 21, 2004.

The Board also directed staff to formally notice the regulations for a 45-day comment period.
