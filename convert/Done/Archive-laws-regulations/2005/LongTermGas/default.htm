Long-Term Landfill Gas Violations

On November 17, 2005, the Office of Administrative Law approved the regulations. The regulations are effective on December 17, 2005.

Description
New regulations establishing compliance procedures for long-term landfill decomposition gas violations.

Regulatory Code Sections That Were Affected
Title 27, California Code of Regulations, Division 2, Subdivision 1, Chapter 4, Subchapter 3, Article 3.1, Section 21685.

Rulemaking History
Board staff conducted a workshop on November 7, 2003, in Sacramento, to solicit comment on informal draft regulation text. At its April 14, 2004 meeting, the Board directed staff to initiate a 45-day public comment period. A 45-day public comment period ran from November 5 through December 22, 2004.

The Board held a public hearing on the proposed regulations on January 10, 2005. After considering comments received during the 45-day comment period and comments made at the public hearing, Board staff modified the proposed regulations. Board staff was directed to initiate an additional 15-day comment period for the proposed changes to the regulations at the Permitting and Enforcement Committee meeting on March 7, 2005.

A 15-day comment period on the revised proposed regulations extended from April 7 through 5:00 p.m. on April 22, 2005.

The Board adopted the regulations at its June 14, 2005, meeting.

On November 17, 2005, the Office of Administrative Law approved the regulations. The regulations are effective on December 17, 2005.
