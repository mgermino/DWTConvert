Electronic Waste Recovery and Recycling

On November 27, 2006, the Office of Administrative Law (OAL) approved the regulations. The regulations became effective immediately.

Description
Regulations that allow the California Department of Resources Recycling and Recovery (CalRecycle)to implement the requirements of the Electronic Waste Recycling Act of 2003 (Act), Chapter 526, Statutes of 2003; as amended by Chapter 863, Statutes of 2004, including the establishment and implementation of a payment system for the collection and proper recycling of covered electronic waste (CEW).

Affected Regulatory Code Sections
Title 14, California Code of Regulations, Division 7, Chapter 8.2, Articles 1 through 5

Status
New final regulations were approved by the Office of Administrative Law (OAL) on November 27, 2006. The regulations were originally adopted by the CIWMB at its Sustainability and Market Development Committee meeting on July 11, 2006, and by the full Board on July 18, 2006.

Note: The CEW recovery and recycling payment system is no longer governed by the emergency regulations.

Contact: Jeff Hunts

Stakeholder Input
Written and oral comments were received by program staff throughout the rulemaking process. Comments and responses are summarized below.

Rulemaking History (in reverse chronological order)

  • New final regulations were approved by the Office of Administrative Law (OAL) on November 27, 2006.
  • The rulemaking package was delivered to the OAL for review on October 13, 2006.
  • The revised proposed regulations were considered and adopted by the CIWMB at its Sustainability and Market Development Committee meeting on July 11, 2006, and by the full Board on July 18, 2006.
  • Revised proposed regulatory language was made available for an additional 15-day comment period. That comment period ended at 5:00 p.m. on June 23, 2006. Only those sections of the proposed regulations that had been revised since March 24, 2006, were subject to comment.
  • Revised proposed regulatory language was heard by the CIWMB's Sustainability and Market Development Committee meeting on June 6, 2006. Staff received direction to make the revised text available for an additional 15-day comment opportunity.
  • A formally noticed 45-day comment period for the originally proposed final regulations ran until May 8, 2006. A public hearing was held in conjunction with the CIWMB's Sustainability and Market Development Committee meeting on May 9, 2006. Staff received direction to consider the comments and to revise the proposed regulations as appropriate.
  • The package containing proposed regulatory language, Initial Statement of Reasons, and the Notice of Proposed Rulemaking was submitted to the Office of Administrative Law (OAL) for publication of the Notice on March 24, 2006. The date of publication began a formal 45-day comment period.
  • Proposed final regulatory language was discussed at the CIWMB's Sustainability and Market Development Committee meeting on February 7, 2006. Program staff received direction to proceed with the formal noticing of the proposed regulations.
  • Revisions to emergency regulations were adopted by the CIWMB at its Board meeting on November 15, 2005, and approved by OAL on December 5, 2005.
  • New emergency regulations were approved by OAL on December 13, 2004, as a result of SB 50 (Sher).
  • Initial emergency regulations approved by OAL in May 2004, to implement the Electronic Waste Recycling Act of 2003 as enacted via SB 20 (Sher)
