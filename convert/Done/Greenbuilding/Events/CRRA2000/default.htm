Conference Information

The theme of this year's California Resource Recovery Association (CRRA) Conference was "Turning Challenges into Opportunities." The conference was held from July 23-26, 2000 at the Sacramento Convention Center. A lunch river boat ride and an evening sunset cruise on the American River were some special events held before the welcome reception on Sunday, July 23rd. A number of tours were organized for Monday, July 24th.There were nine tracks on the 25th and 26th to address the year 2000 theme. Track 7 was "Sustainability: Green Building and Beyond Recycling."
