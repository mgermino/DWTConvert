Sustainable (Green) Building: Programs and Partnerships

California Green Building Collaborative

This program is no longer active, but some of this information may have some value to green builders, so it has been provided for historical reference.

A number of local governments have joined together to form the California Green Building Collaborative. These cities and counties are working together to avoid duplicating efforts, pool resources, and act as an information exchange in developing their green building programs. Some of the local governments involved in the collaborative have established programs including the development of local green building guidelines, an awards program, and incentives. Please follow the links below for information related to their programs:
