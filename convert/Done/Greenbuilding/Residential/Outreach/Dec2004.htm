Sustainable (Green) Building

Southern California Outreach
In December 2004, CIWMB Sustainable Building staff held a workshop in Downey, California to discuss voluntary, statewide residential green building guidelines and to start coordination of a regional approach in Southern California jurisdictions.

This workshop was attended by representatives from four counties. The following information was presented at that workshop.
