Sustainable (Green) Building

Local Government Training

Workshop Presentation

The California Integrated Waste Management Board and the Department of General Services are developing a sustainable design workshop for local governments in partnership with HDR Architecture. This document is a draft of one section of this workshop that is intended to provide attendees with a basic understanding of sustainable design. This draft has been posted on the CIWMB website to allow individuals to review the materials in a zero waste manner.
