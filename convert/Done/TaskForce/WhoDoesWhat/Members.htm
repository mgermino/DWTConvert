Sustainable (Green) Building

Task Force Representatives

The multi-agency, interdisciplinary Sustainable Building Task Force was convened by former Secretary of the State and Consumer Services Agency, Aileen Adams to recommend strategies to incorporate sustainable building practices in the development of State facilities. The Task Force last worked to provide technical resources to implement Governor Schwarzenegger's Executive Order S-20-04 and to provide green building resources for state construction projects. Since the formation of the Green Action Team, the Task Force no longer meets. The following is provided as a historical record of their efforts.
