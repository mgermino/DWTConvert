Sustainable (Green) Building

State Programs

Several State agencies that participated in the now defunct task force have programs that affect the way we design and operate State buildings. The following State programs contribute to the statewide sustainable building effort.

  • Department of Health Services, California Interagency Working Group on Indoor Air Quality
  • Department of Transportation, Caltrans' Resource Conservation Program
  • California State University, Capital Planning, Design, and Construction
