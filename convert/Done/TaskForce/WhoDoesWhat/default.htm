Sustainable (Green) Building

Who's Doing What

This page has been developed to provide information about what organizations are already doing to implement sustainable building design into their projects. In addition, there is a link to a list of events that are taking place to assist in further education.

Green Action Team: Cabinet-level group responsible for overseeing the efforts to green State building projects and to implement Executive Order S-20-04.

Programs and Partnerships: Links to information on sustainable building programs and partnerships, including: government programs, home building programs, nonprofit organizations, international organizations, K-12 schools, and higher education.

Events: Links to upcoming sustainable building events.
