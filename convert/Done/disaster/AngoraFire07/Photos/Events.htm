Disaster Preparedness and Response: Photos

Events: 2007 Angora Wildfire Cleanup (Lake Tahoe)

Select any image to view a larger version.

 

   

Above: On August 17, 2007, Lieutenant Governor John Garamendi paid a visit to the Angora Fire Debris Removal Command Center to hear firsthand the progress being made on the recovery and rebuild efforts. The Lt. Governor was joined by Secretary for Resources Mike Chrisman, Office of Emergency Services Director Henry Renteria, El Dorado County Supervisors Helen Baumann and Norma Santiago, and City of South Lake Tahoe Mayor Kathay Lovell. The Lt. Governor was briefed on how the debris removal process is working and the next phase of recovery as well as hearing from several residents and victims of the fire.
 

Appreciation Lunch for Crews of the Angora Debris Removal Incident (August 9, 2007). Far left: The entire Angora Debris Removal Incident crew gathers outside the operations center following the much-deserved appreciation barbeque that was provided by AJ Diani. Left: Crews and guests applaud comments made by Operations Chief Todd Thalhamer.
 

July 20, 2007 press conference: CIWMB's Todd Thalhamer shares with Angora Fire-area residents and the press about progress and processes regarding the debris cleanup.
