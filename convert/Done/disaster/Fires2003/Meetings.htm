FEMA Meetings for Local and State Government Concerning Eligibility for Disaster Cleanup Funding

Riverside County

1:00 p.m. Public Assistance Program

San Diego County
9:30 a.m. Public Assistance Program
San Diego Operational Area
Emergency Operations Center
5555 Overland Blvd.
Building 19, East EOC Room
San Diego, CA 92123
For directions call (858) 565-3490

Thursday, November 20
State Agencies
10:00 a.m. Public Assistance Program
Governor's Office of Emergency Services
3650 Schriever Ave.
East Multipurpose Room
Mather, CA 95655
For directions call (916) 845-8245
