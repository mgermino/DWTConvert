School DEEL

SB 373--The School DEEL

In September 2001, the Governor signed into law the School Diversion and Environmental Education Law (DEEL), formerly referred to as Senate Bill 373 (Torlakson, Chapter 926, Statutes of 2001), which creates many more program mandates for the Board's Office of Education and the Environment and Office of Local Assistance.

Please visit the School DEEL home page for much more information.

If you are interested in how the program got started:

  • View updates on implementation from 2002-2003.
