Unified Education Strategy Grant Program
The Unified Education Strategy (UES) Grant Program coordinated instructional resources with onsite conservation programs, promoted service learning, and assessed the impact of these efforts as they related both to student achievement and resource conservation. The grant program began in summer 2003 and concluded in spring 2005.
