Federal Legislation

CIWMB Top Ten 1997 Federal Bills

 
The following bills were on the California Integrated Waste Management Board's top ten list of federal legislation being considered in Congress.  You can check the final status of these and other federal bills in the Final Status Report from the 1997-1998 Session of the California Legislature and the 105th U.S. Congress. Download this document from our on-line publications section or order a free hard copy.

HR 943 (Franks, Bob, R-NJ): Municipal Solid Waste Flow Control Act of 1997.

HR 1041 (Kennedy, Patrick, D-RI): Solid Waste Disposal Act, Amendment (Tires).

HR 1206 (Visclosky, D-IN): EPA Program of Voluntary State Environmental Cleanups.

HR 1586 (Rivers, D-MI): National Beverage Container Reuse and Recycling Act of 1997. (Companion bill to S 215 (Jeffords, R-VT)

S 8 (Smith, Robert C., R-NH):  Superfund Cleanup Acceleration Act of 1997.

S 215 (Jeffords, R-VT):National Beverage Container Reuse and Recycling Act of 1997.  (Companion bill to HR 1586 (Rivers, D-MI)

S 444 (Chafee, R-RI): Internal Revenue Code of 1986, Amendment (Tires).

S 445 (Chafee, R-RI): Waste Tire Recycling, Abatement, and Disposal Act of 1997.

S 448 (Robb, D-VA): Solid Waste Disposal Act, Amendment.

S 463 (Coats, R-IN): Solid Waste Disposal Act, Amendment.
