AB 117 (Escutia)
Solid Waste: Tire Fees

Description:  Extends the sunset date for the California Tire Recycling Act, including the operation of fee provisions ($0.25 per tire, upon purchase), from June 30, 1999 to January 1, 2001.  AB 117 requires a waste tire report on specified matters relating to tire recycling and recommendations for legislation to be submitted to the Legislature and the Governor by June 30, 1999.

Status: Chaptered by the Secretary of State on 9/30/98; Chapter 1020, Statutes of 1998

Contact: Barbara Peavy (916) 255-2417, fax (916) 255-2644

Related Documents:

AB 228 (Migden)
Solid Waste: Tires

Description:  Adds abandonment of tires to the circumstances under which a person can be convicted of a crime; allows a city, county or city and county to request designation to exercise enforcement authority from the CIWMB; allows penalties collected from waste tire violations to be retained by the city, county, or city and county if the attorney who brought the action represents that local government entity; and requires moneys retained by the city, county, or city and county to be expended on enforcement and cleanup required under this chapter.

Status: Chaptered by the Secretary of State on 9/30/98; Chapter 1019, Statutes of 1998

Contact: Barbara Peavy (916) 255-2417, fax (916) 255-2644

AB 715 (Figueroa)
Solid Waste Facilities: Closure: Financial Ability

Description:  Allows a solid waste facility operator who owns an insurance company to insure itself for landfill closure and post-closure care, if approved by the CIWMB and if specified requirements are met.

AB 964 (Bowen)
Solid Waste: Tires

Description: Would have allowed the CIWMB to obtain access to a site where tires are unlawfully housed for remediation purposes when the situation presents a significant threat to public health or the environment. AB 964 would have required the CIWMB, in conjunction with the State Budget, to submit a yearly report describing expenditures proposed to be made for grants, loans and contracts under the tire recycling program. Additionally, the bill would have modified the amount of civil penalties for violations of those provisions and allow a city, county, or city and county, to request designation as an enforcement authority for waste tires.

Status: Vetoed by Governor Wilson on 9/30/98

Contact: Barbara Peavy (916) 255-2417, fax (916) 255-2644

AB 2432 (Bowen)
State Buildings and Publicly Funded Schools: Standards ("Green" Buildings)

Description: Would, after January 1, 2000, have required all new State public buildings for which design and construction begins after that date to exceed the minimum building energy-efficiency standards mandated by the California Building Code and would have required such buildings to follow green building standards and utilize green building measures, materials, and devices, as specified.

Status: Vetoed by Governor Wilson on 9/30/98

AB 2521 (Wayne)
Solid Waste Facilities

Description: Would have permitted local enforcement agencies (LEAs) to recover costs for defending themselves against appeals of denials of a solid waste facility’s permit where they prevail, eliminated the prohibition against a member of an independent hearing panel from serving more than two consecutive two-year terms, and provided that a cease and desist order issued, under certain circumstances, by an LEA against a solid waste facility operator remain in force and effect while any appeal by the operator is pending.

Status: Vetoed by Governor Wilson on 9/21/98

SB 1340 (Polanco)
Solid Waste Enterprise: Indemnity Obligation

Description:  Imposes specified restrictions on the enforceability of an indemnity obligation contained in any provision, term, condition, or requirement in an ordinance, contract, franchise, license, permit, or other entitlement or right adopted, entered into, issued, or granted, as the case may be, by a local agency for solid waste collection and handling, including the recycling, processing, or composting of solid waste, or in any request for bids or proposal in connection with any such contract or franchise.
